About

Composer performer Austin Gray Williams writes music that reflects the diverse amount of musical experiences and how they have influenced him along the way.

Much of Williams’ music has a tendency to follow traditional aspects of harmony and melody while also incorporating other elements from varying degrees of styles such as jazz, minimalism, experimentalism and traditional art music.

Influences and philosophies are apt to change everyday and it’s whether or not we accept them into our approach or not.
