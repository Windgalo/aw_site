Music

Austin's pursuit in composition is heavily influenced by the inherent need to perform. The two share a symbiotic relation to one another and would be remiss without one side of it. Williams specializes in performances of new music and creates an opportunity to write for an otherwise non soloistic instrument.

Performances

Austin is interested in working with contemporary artists in performances of new music that push the boundaries of instruments and their musicians.
